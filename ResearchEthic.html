Research Ethics


i. Limited Availability of Resources

The first challenge I faced was the limited availability of relevant and reliable sources of information. I realized the importance of conducting a comprehensive literature review to identify gaps and determine the most credible sources. I meet my challenge by expanding my search strategies, exploring various databases, and reaching out to experts in the field to access relevant and reliable information.

ii. Managing and Organizing Large Volumes of Data

I have difficulties managing and organizing large volumes of research data, but I understood the significance of effective data management and organization for efficient analysis and retrieval. Hence, I employed research management tools and developed a systematic approach to categorize, store, and analyze data, ensuring easy access and efficient handling of the large dataset.

iii. Defining Clear Research Objectives

The next challenge that I faced was the difficulty in narrowing down the research focus and defining clear research objectives. I learned the importance of clearly defining research objectives and narrowing down the focus to ensure a cohesive and meaningful study and conducted extensive background research, sought feedback from mentors, and refined my research questions to establish a clear direction for my study.

iv. Time Constraints

In addition to that, I faced time constraints and balancing research with other responsibilities. I recognized the significance of effective time management and prioritization to meet research deadlines while balancing other commitments, so I created a research schedule, set realistic goals, and allocated dedicated time for different research tasks, ensuring a balance between research and other responsibilities.


I discovered the importance of investing time in comprehending complex concepts and methodologies to ensure accurate interpretation and application when dealing with complex or technical concepts that require deeper understanding. Hence, I engaged in extensive reading, attended seminars and workshops, and sought guidance from experts in the field to deepen my understanding and grasp complex concepts effectively.

I understood the significance of rigor and transparency in research to ensure the validity and reliability of findings to ensure the validity and accuracy of research findings. So, I implemented robust research methodologies, collected data meticulously, maintained detailed documentation, and conducted a thorough analysis to enhance the credibility of my research.


Preventing plagiarism is essential for maintaining academic integrity and ensuring that research is based on original work. Hence these are the few steps that I have taken to prevent plagiarism.

i. Familiarize with Various Form of Plagiarism

As I understood what constitutes plagiarism, I familiarize myself with the various forms of plagiarism, which includes avoiding direct copying without proper citation, paraphrasing without attribution, and self-plagiarism. Next, I planned my research and writing process, which can prevent unintentional plagiarism by creating a research plan that includes identifying my research goals, gathering relevant sources, and keeping track of all the sources consulted. When I want to incorporate ideas or information from a source into my own writing, I practice effective paraphrasing and summarizing techniques by expressing the ideas in my own words.

i. Familiarize with Various Form of Plagiarism

I am aware to use proper citation and referencing especially when using ideas, information, or direct quotes from a source, and then cite and reference it properly. I also ensure to follow the appropriate citation style guidelines to ensure consistency and accuracy in my citations and to include in-text citations within my writing and to provide a comprehensive reference list or bibliography at the end of my work. For additional measures, I utilized a plagiarism detection tool to check my work for any instances of unintentional plagiarism. These tools have helped to compare my writing to a vast database of published works and identify any similarities that may indicate plagiarism, and after reviewing these results, I make the necessary revisions to avoid plagiarism.

iii. Seeking Guidance

As the final measure, I seek guidance and ask for help especially when unsure about proper citation practices or need assistance with paraphrasing or summarizing, consult writing guides, style manuals, or academic writing resources. I have reached out to third parties to further clarify on plagiarism prevention.
